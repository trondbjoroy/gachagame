The throne is empty.

When the last Sovereign died without an heir, the realm's champions were bound into soulstone and their fates written into the Great Ledger, a book no lord can burn and no scribe can forge. Now the banners are yours to raise: summon champions no one else will ever own, work them in the deep mines, prove them in the Pit, and trade them for gold in the Bazaar. Every deed is witnessed forever.

🔥

SUMMON

The Blind Weaver binds a champion into soulstone with every summoning: one of a kind in all the realm, its station and power struck at birth. No two are alike, and no one can conjure a copy.

⛏️

MINE

Send champions into the deep mines, where soulstone sweats gems by the minute. Gems forge greater bloodlines, temper your champions' aspects, and back every wager in the Pit.

⚔️

FIGHT

Trial by combat, best of three: valor, bulwark, and guile. Wager gems, read your opponent's aspects, and let veterans carry their victories on the card forever.

🪙

TRADE

Sell champions for coin or strike sworn trades in the Bazaar. A ten-victory veteran is provably a ten-victory veteran; pedigree travels with the card.
